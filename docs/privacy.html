Privacy Policy

Monkey Fart Madness · Last updated 17 July 2026

The short version: Monkey Fart Madness collects nothing, sends nothing, and has no way to. The app contains no networking code at all.

What we collect

Nothing. We do not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal information. There are no accounts, no sign-in, no email capture and no contact form.

What we track

Nothing. The app contains no analytics, no advertising SDKs, no third-party SDKs of any kind, and no tracking identifiers. It never asks for permission to track you, because it has nothing to track you with.

Data stored on your device

The game saves your progress so it's there next time you play. This stays on your device, in the app's own private storage, and is never transmitted anywhere:

  • Your best score
  • Banana Coins — current balance and lifetime total (your rank)
  • Hats and fart-colours you've unlocked, and which are equipped
  • Your chosen world, difficulty, control style, and music setting
  • Which day you last claimed the Fart of the Day bonus

None of this identifies you. Deleting the app deletes all of it.

Permissions

The app requests no permissions. It does not access the camera, microphone, photos, contacts, location, health data, or any other sensitive resource.

Money

There are no advertisements and no in-app purchases. Nothing in this game costs real money, and nothing ever will. Every hat, colour and rank is earned by playing.

Children

This game was built for a six-year-old, and it's designed so a child can play it without anyone worrying. Because it collects no data whatsoever, there is nothing to collect from a child — which puts it comfortably inside COPPA and the GDPR's rules on children's data. There are no links out of the app, no chat, no user-generated content, and no way to contact another player.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this address with a new date. If a future version of the app ever collected anything, that would be stated here plainly and before it shipped.
