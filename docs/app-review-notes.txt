3. PURPOSE AND TARGET AUDIENCE
Monkey Fart Madness is a single-player arcade dodge game for children (roughly
ages 5-10) and the adults playing alongside them. It was written for the
developer's own six-year-old son.

Gameplay: cartoon monkeys hang from a branch and throw bananas at the player.
The player moves left and right to dodge, and fights back two ways - a rising
gas cloud, or throwing a banana back. Ten levels and a boss.

The value it provides is a genuinely child-appropriate arcade game with no ads,
no in-app purchases, no accounts, no chat, no user-generated content and no data
collection. Everything unlockable is earned by playing. There is nothing to buy
and no way to spend real money anywhere in the app.

4. HOW TO SET UP AND REACH EVERY FEATURE
No login, account, credentials or sample files are required. The app opens
directly into playable content. From the title screen:

- Difficulty/mode: the row of three buttons above PLAY - "Free Play" (a no-fail
  sandbox: the player cannot lose), "Easy" (6 lives), "Hard" (4 lives).
- Start playing: tap the large PLAY button.
- Art style: tap any of the four large world buttons across the bottom (Loud!,
  Doodle, Inkwell, Plasticine). Each is a complete art and audio theme.
- Shop: cosmetic hats, fart colours and a Shield power-up, bought with "Banana
  Coins" earned by playing. These are NOT in-app purchases and cannot be bought
  with money.
- Settings: control style (Zones or Buttons), plus separate Music and sound
  effect switches.
- In-game controls (default "Zones"): hold the left or right side of the lower
  screen to move - both touch areas are outlined with dotted lines. Tap either
  side to jump, tap again mid-air to double jump. The banana button throws; the
  cloud button releases gas. If a Shield has been bought, its button appears
  beside them: single tap cycles the selected power-up, double tap activates it.
- Pause/quit a run: the pause button, top-left.

To reach the boss quickly, choose Easy and play through the ten levels.

5. EXTERNAL SERVICES, TOOLS OR PLATFORMS
None. The app uses no third-party services or SDKs of any kind. No data
providers, authentication, payment processing, analytics, advertising or AI
services.

The app contains no networking code at all - no URLSession, no web view, no
socket use anywhere in the binary - so it cannot send or receive data even in
principle. It uses only Apple system frameworks (UIKit, Core Graphics,
AVFoundation, CoreHaptics). All progress is stored locally in UserDefaults and
never leaves the device. Hence the "Data Not Collected" privacy declaration.

6. REGIONAL DIFFERENCES
None. The app behaves identically in every region. No region-specific content,
no server, no remote configuration, nothing that varies by locale. English only.

7. REGULATED INDUSTRY OR THIRD-PARTY MATERIAL
Not applicable. All content - artwork, code, music and sound - is original work
by the developer. Artwork is drawn procedurally in code and music is synthesised
at runtime. No licensed or third-party material is included.

ADDITIONAL NOTES
The humour is cartoon flatulence and slapstick. Nothing is harmed on screen;
monkeys are briefly stunned and recover. There are no purpose strings because
the app requests no permissions - no camera, microphone, location, contacts,
photos or tracking.
