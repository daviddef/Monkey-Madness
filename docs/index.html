The whole game in one sentence

A branch full of monkeys moons you and parps bananas in your direction. Don't get hit — and get them back. It's a dodge game built belly-laughs first, for kids and the adults laughing next to them.

💨
Fart back

Costs gas. A rising cloud that eats bananas on the way up and gasses any monkey it drifts into. Slow and wide — you aim it by standing under them.

🍌
Throw a banana

Costs ammo. Fast, auto-aimed, a reliable bonk. And the ammo comes from them: bananas that land in one piece become pickups.

⤒
Jump, double jump, hover

Tap to fart-jump. Tap again mid-air to double jump. Or hold as you fall and ride your own gas down.

🛡️
Earn it, don't buy it

Bonk monkeys for Banana Coins, spend them on hats, fart colours and power-ups. Every unlock is earned by playing.

Built for a six-year-old

A promise, not a marketing line.
  • No ads. Not banners, not videos, not "watch to continue".
  • No in-app purchases. Nothing in this game costs real money, and nothing ever will.
  • No accounts, no sign-in, no chat — and no links out of the app.
  • No tracking or analytics. The app has no networking code at all; your progress never leaves the device. See the privacy policy.
  • Free Play mode — a no-fail sandbox where nothing can go wrong, for when a small player just wants to mess about.
  • Two control styles and an Easy mode with a genuinely gentler barrage, not just extra hearts.
